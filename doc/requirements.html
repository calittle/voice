Functional Requirement List

  • Create a user
  • Create a registrant (voter) and tie to user
  • Initial registrant requires review and approval.
  • Approval is conditional on mapping to one or more districts, having a validated residence, and accepting affirmations for residence state.
  • Registrant may belong to one or more districts, provide ability to link and update as required as districts may be redrawn.
  • Manage districts (add, delete)
  • Retrieve and display affirmations for acceptance.
  • Display registrant information in various levels of detail
  • Associate no more than two locations with a registrant: mailing address and residence
  • Remove a location from a registrant.
  • Create an election and associate one or more measures, each measure with one or more options.
  • Provide start and end date/time with an election and ability to extend or change the time.
  • Correlate an election to one or more districts so as to restrict voting in the election only to registrants in associated districts
  • List elections for a registrant according to districts.
  • Display measures and options for an election
  • Record choices of registrant for election/measure/option. Note: only during start/end windows!
  • Record digital signature of registrant when casting a ballot
  • Display receipt of cast ballot.
  • Use CAPTCHA to prevent robovoting
